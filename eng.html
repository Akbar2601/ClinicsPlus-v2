Why Choose ClinicsPlus?

ClinicsPlus is an innovative solution for finding medical services and clinics. We have gathered a database of all clinics in your city with up-to-date information on hours, services, and prices. With convenient filters, you can find the right specialist, compare service costs, and book an appointment in just a few clicks.

  • • Search for clinics near you.
  • • Online appointment booking.
  • • Price comparison.
  • • Available for free for users.

Benefits for Users and Clinics

Benefits for Users:

  • • Clinic locations on the map.

  • • Filtering by price, types of services, and reviews.

  • • Appointment booking for tests and consultations (in the paid version).

Benefits for Clinics:

  • • Admin panel for managing services and prices.

  • • Increased visibility among users.

  • • Online appointment booking for patients (in the premium version).
